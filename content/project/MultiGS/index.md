# Gaussian-based Dynamic Reconstruction of Multi-person Monocular Videos

March 2024 - Present

Research Intern at Tsinghua University, Advisor: Prof. Shi-Min Hu and Tai-Jiang Mu

* Integrated the human-specific gaussian-splatting and confidence-guided optimization formulation to reconstruct multiple individuals moving and interacting naturally from monocular in-the-wild videos
* Utilized the Segment-Anything-2 method to enhance the reconstruction process, achieving high-resolution, pixel-level markers for precise tracking
* Reproduced the HUGS model by Apple Intelligence and integrated it into a modular neural network framework with plug-ins, enhancing the model’s adaptability to varying data complexities
* Achieved 200x acceleration in time and 1/8 training device demand while succeeding in reconstructing detailed dynamic models of equal rendering image quality
